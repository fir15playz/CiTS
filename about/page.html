Meet our founders

John Nichols

Co-founder & lead strategist

John has over 5 years experience in sustainability and strategy. Hes helped companies from tech to manufacturing create actionable net-zero plans. John focuses on making climate strategy clear and empowering SMEs to lead with confidence.

Laura Hall

Co-founder & technical director
